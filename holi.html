Holi Google Doodle
Google · Motion design
Holi is an ancient Hindu festival that celebrates the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil. It is also the festival of colors and participants often throw vibrant colored powders in the air and onto each other.

When this Doodle launched, there was also an interactive version that allowed users tap on the Google logo to paint on their own pattern of colors
