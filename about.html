Hello! Thanks for visiting and taking the time to look look at my work.
I'm Diana, a designer currently living in San Francisco with an aggressive monstera plant named Jenova. These days I mostly design fun and delightful interactive experiences with the Google Doodle team but I still do a fair share of product design.

I'm currently teaching myself 3D Modeling and Animation. I also do a lot of front-end development. In fact, this site is 100% lovingly hand coded. (please let me know if you spot a bug!)
